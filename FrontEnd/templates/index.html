Data Visualization

In this website you will be able to gain insights on various aspects of the value chain of H&M. The data has been provided from the company and has been uploaded to Google Cloud and MongoDb in order to be secured in the cloud . By exploring the articles, customer and transactions part of the website you will be presented with graphs and KPIs that give some insights on the portfolio of H&M. If you want to search for your own insights and find your own KPIs you should check out the play with datasets part of the website.

Good luck with your findings!
